=== System Call Numbers ===

Syscall numbers on this architecture:
SYS_getpid  = 39
SYS_getppid = 110
SYS_write   = 1

Calling syscalls by number:
My PID (via syscall 39): [0-9]+
Parent PID (via syscall 110): [0-9]+

Using wrapper functions:
My PID (via getpid):  [0-9]+
Parent PID (via getppid): [0-9]+

Wrapper functions internally use syscall numbers!
